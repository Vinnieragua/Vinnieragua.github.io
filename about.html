About the Artist

Artist Statement

As a Performing Engineer, I inhabit the intersection of technical precision and artistic expression. My work transforms discarded objects and found materials into interactive musical installations that challenge conventional boundaries between art, technology, and performance.

I believe that beauty exists in the overlooked - in the broken circuits, abandoned machinery, and forgotten objects that surround us. Through my performances, I give these materials new voice and new purpose, creating symphonies from scrap and poetry from pollution.

My practice is rooted in sustainability and improvisation. Each installation is a unique dialogue between materials, environment, and audience, where technical engineering meets spontaneous artistic creation. The result is an ever-evolving exploration of sound, movement, and human interaction with the technological world we've left behind.
